Upon combining (2.25) and (2.28) one ends up with

 $$ \beta(t)=\frac{\dot{\mu_{0}}(t)}{\mu_{1}(t)}\;. $$ 

This is just (1.19), the relation given by BAMFORD and TOMPA [1]. At the same time, the above derivation explains the observations of these authors that “the more closely the molecular weight distribution approximates to the simple exponential distribution, the smaller the number of moments required” [1].
