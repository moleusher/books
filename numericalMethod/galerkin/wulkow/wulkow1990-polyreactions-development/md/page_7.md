A number of typical macromolecular reaction steps (chain addition, transfer reactions, termination processes) is implemented. Due to the modular character of the method, additional steps can easily be added (if analytically preprocessable). The size of a chemical system, determined by the number of polymer species, other chemical species and reaction steps, is only restricted by the available computer memory.

Note, that the complete reaction mechanism is checked in view of stoichiometric plausibility.

As illustrated in [1], realistic problems can be solved with MACRON, e.g. free radical polymerization, soot formation in flames and plasma etching in semi conductor production.
