## Self-Education Summary

Self-education has enormous potential if you are willing to pursue it. Getting the right habits and creating a structure for learning can be initially difficult, but afterwards those habits can be put to use towards any goal you might have.

I’ve collected various resources for self-education you might find useful to get started:

MIT OpenCourseWare – A collection of free courses online offered by MIT.

 $ \underline{\text{EHow.com}} $ - How to articles on a variety of subjects.

 $ \underline{\text{FreeEd.net}} $ - "Free education on the Internet" according to their website.

 $ \underline{\text{Portal to Free Online Courses}} $ – This article links to many major universities which offer free courses online.
