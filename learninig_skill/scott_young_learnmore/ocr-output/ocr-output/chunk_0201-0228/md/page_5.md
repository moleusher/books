4) Pick a time. I like to do morning reading, because it gives me uninterrupted quiet hours where I can focus my thoughts on a particular subject. Picking a specific time each day will reinforce your habit.

If you aren’t sure where to find the time to do daily reading or practice, ask yourself how much television you watch or web surfing you do. If you are serious about learning on your own and can’t seem to find the time, TV and StumbleUpon need to be the first things to go.
