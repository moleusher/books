## My Story

I’ve always been able to learn quickly. Getting A’s and A+’s with little studying before tests wasn’t a challenge for me throughout school. While in University, I’ve maintained an average that sits between A and A+. Despite this, I don’t spend more than the average person on homework. In fact, I might even spend less.

Once, I wrote an inter-provincial test (I'm Canadian) for chemistry. The only problem: I didn't know I was supposed to write the test until a pencil and bubble sheet were sitting in front of me. On top of this, the test was on material I wasn't familiar with and topics that were never covered in my class. I was given an hour and a half to write the exam. I left after forty minutes because I wanted to eat lunch.

I won first place and received a check for $400.
