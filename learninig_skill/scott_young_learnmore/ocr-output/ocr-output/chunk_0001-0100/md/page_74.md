## Opinion Information

Opinion information is information you need to argue. Arguing whether Theravada Buddhism is superior to Mahayana Buddhism would require learning opinion information. This type of information is common in essays and presentations in school. This type of information is also important when making decisions where there isn't a consensus (what foods are healthy, what business opportunities are good, etc.).

## Challenges With Opinion Information

With opinion information, your biggest problem is the acquiring phase. You need to examine a large volume of information and look for patterns, rather than memorize specific details. Speed reading techniques are a great asset in gathering information.

Diagraming is also a useful method with opinion information. It can help you distill your key ideas after reading.
