As I'll explain, seeing how these three elements fit together makes applying the holistic learning strategy easier. Just as knowing the different chess plays is useless without knowing that a bishop moves diagonally, knowing the holistic learning strategy is useless unless we both operate from the same points of reference.

## Quick Tip!

Holistic learning is based on linking ideas as a way of remembering and applying them. The best techniques to start learning are metaphor, visceralization, flow-based notetaking and diagraming. These methods form the foundation of holistic learning.
