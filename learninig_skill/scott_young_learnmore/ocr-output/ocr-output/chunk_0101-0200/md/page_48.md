## Quick Tip!

One attribute the Link method shares with holistic learning methods is the need for imagination. Don't be shy, trying to pick only "reasonable" connections between ideas. Linking works best when the mental pictures are exaggerated and bizarre. Holistic learning works best with many, varied connections.
