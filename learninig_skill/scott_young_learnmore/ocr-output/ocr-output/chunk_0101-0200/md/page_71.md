## Mind Challenge!

Once a week for the next two months, pick one of your major courses. Spend at least 5-10 minutes creating a list of ways you can use this information in your daily life. Make sure the list has at least 20 ideas and don't censor yourself in letting them flow. After you're done this weekly exercise, pick one of those ideas and implement it.
