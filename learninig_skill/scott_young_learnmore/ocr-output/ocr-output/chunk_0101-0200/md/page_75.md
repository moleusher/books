## 3 ) Spread Practice Times Out

Split your practice sessions into daily intervals, rather than pre-test crash sessions. A quick refresh each day will be more likely to stick than a huge download into your brain the night before.

## Quick Tip!

Practicing questions repeatedly is a slow way to learn. Model debugging is necessary, but if you need to do the same types of questions repeatedly, chances are you need more work on the exploring or understanding phases. If you feel you don't quite understand a topic, try going back and exploring the ideas.
