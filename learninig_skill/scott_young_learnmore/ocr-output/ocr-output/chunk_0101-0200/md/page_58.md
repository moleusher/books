## Advanced Pegging

An advanced form of using the peg technique involves associating a phonetic sound to each digit 0-9, rather than a rhyming image. You can then use the phonetic sound to form a series of words to store dozens, if not hundreds of numbers in a sequence instead of just a couple. I won’t go into this form of pegging, simply because I’ve found it to be unnecessary for more than party tricks.

However, if you want to impress your friends with your ability to remember a string of hundreds of numbers, you might want to do a search looking for more information on this advanced form of the peg technique.
