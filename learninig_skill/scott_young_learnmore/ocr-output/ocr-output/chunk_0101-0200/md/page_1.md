These three questions force you to move each major point through the understanding, explore and finally application phase of holistic learning.

For example, let's say I just finished reading a chapter in psychology about classical conditioning. The major points might be:

- Discovered by Ivan Pavlov

- Connects a stimulus with a response

I might remember this by:

- Picturing Pavlov's dogs which drooled at the sound of a bell.

I might extend this by:

- Remembering how I feel compelled to answer the phone if I hear a similar ringtone on a television program.
