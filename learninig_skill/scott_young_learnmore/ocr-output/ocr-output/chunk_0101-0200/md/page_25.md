Visceralizing this process of calculating a determinant involved first imagining a  $ 2 \times 2 $ grid containing four numbers. I then imagined my right hand swiping through the top left and bottom right numbers. As I did this I imagined a blue streak and the feeling of added weight being formed in my hand. Then I imagined my left hand swiping through the top right and bottom left numbers. From that I imagined a red streak and weight being reduced in my right hand.
