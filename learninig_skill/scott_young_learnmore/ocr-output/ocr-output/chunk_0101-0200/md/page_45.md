Turning a formula into a sequence requires that you convert it into a form you might enter into a calculator. Therefore a formula such as V = 1+x/b could be written as V = 1 PLUS X DIVIDE B. V, =, 1, PLUS, X, DIVIDE and B would be your elements in the series. Complex formulas may take time to break down if you aren’t used to this approach, but it can still be helpful if you are worried you might forget.
