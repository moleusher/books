# Productivity Tip #5 Be Organized

Being organized will not get you A's. I've known A+ students who have horrible organization skills and C students with flawless systems. Being organized can help with your productivity and learning, but don't assume that all your problems have disorganization at their root.

If you haven't already, here are the key steps necessary for staying organized:

1) Everything has a home. Tasks, assignments and due dates all get stored in a proper location. Mess is the result of the homeless. Devise a place to put everything, and it will be a lot easier to stay ordered.
