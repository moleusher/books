## Mind Challenge!

Design a project that you expect to take one month to accomplish. Examples could include:

-E-book

-Invention

-Program

-Design

-Website

-Blog

The project should be simple, but have a sharp learning curve. Write out your deadline for completion and schedule a bit of time each day to work on this project. After you’ve finished the project do a review of the project-based learning method and decide what other projects you might like to tackle.
