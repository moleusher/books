# Tips for Improving Your Metaphors

The problem with both of the examples in the last section is that they are based on previous metaphors I’ve already constructed. When you are given an idea, you might have few starting points in creating a metaphor. How can you improve the speed and quality of the metaphors you make?

## 1 ) Start by asking for a metaphor

Unless you ask yourself what a good metaphor would be to describe an idea, you probably won't find one. Keep the concept of metaphors in mind the next time you encounter an idea you have difficulty relating to.
