# RMG — Reaction Mechanism Generator

**URL:** https://rmg.mit.edu/

## Overview

RMG is an open-source software package that automatically builds chemical reaction kinetic models. It constructs these models "using a general understanding of how molecules react," assembling them from elementary reaction steps.

The software is developed collaboratively by the **William H. Green** research group at MIT's Department of Chemical Engineering and the **Richard H. West** research group at Northeastern University's Department of Chemical Engineering. It is distributed under the **MIT/X11 license** and is free to use. The latest stable version is **4.0.0**.

## Website Features

This interactive site provides tools for visualizing and exploring RMG-generated models:

- **Documentation** — guides for using the RMG software
- **Resources** — related publications, presentations, and citation information
- **Database** — browse chemical and kinetic parameters
- **Molecule Search** — view molecules, adjacency lists, and search properties
- **Kinetics Search** — look up kinetics data for chemical reactions
- **Solvation Tools** — search solvation properties for solvent/solute pairs
- **Pressure Dependent Networks** — Arkane pressure-dependent kinetic calculations
- **Other RMG Tools** — supplementary utilities
